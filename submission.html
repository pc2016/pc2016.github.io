    Submission


    Submission of papers and abstracts is now closed.

    Accepted papers must be presented at the workshop. The proceedings will be published as a volume of Electronic Proceedings in Theoretical Computer Science (EPTCS) before the workshop. Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit revised and extended versions of their papers, subject to additional review, for publication in a special issue of the International Journal of Unconventional Computing (IJUC).

    Registration


    PC2016 is part of the broader UCNC conference and all costs are included in registration for the primary conference. We thus invite you to register for the conference here: http://www.ucnc2016.org/.

    Important Dates


    Submission deadline: 8 April 2016 (Extended)
    Notification of acceptance: 17 May 2016
    Final version of papers due: 10 June 2016

    Camera-Ready Versions of Accepted Papers


    Authors of accepted papers are invited to prepare the final version of their papers by 10 June at the latest. Please take into account the comments of the referees when revising your paper, making sure to prepare it in LaTeX following the EPTCS style guide, and taking note of the 12-page limit.

    We invite authors of accepted papers to register by following the instructions above.
