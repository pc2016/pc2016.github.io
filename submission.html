The 7th International Workshop on

Physics and Computation 2016


A satellite worksop to UCNC 2016, Manchester, UK, 14 July 2016

    Schedule


    The workshop will take place on Thursday the 14th of July. The schedule for the UCNC conference, including the Physics and Computation Workshop, is available here.

    Local Information and Venue


    For information on the conference venue, how to get there, or any other local information, please visit the UCNC website.

    Submission


    Submission of papers and abstracts is now closed.

    Accepted papers must be presented at the workshop. The proceedings will be published as a volume of Electronic Proceedings in Theoretical Computer Science (EPTCS) before the workshop. Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit revised and extended versions of their papers, subject to additional review, for publication in a special issue of the International Journal of Unconventional Computing (IJUC).

    Registration


    PC2016 is part of the broader UCNC conference and all costs are included in registration for the primary conference. We thus invite you to register for the conference here: http://www.ucnc2016.org/. Registration is now closed.

    Important Dates


    Submission deadline: 8 April 2016 (Extended)
    Notification of acceptance: 17 May 2016
    Final version of papers due: 10 June 2016
    Workshop date: 14 July 2016
