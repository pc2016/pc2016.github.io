    Submission


    Authors are invited to submit original papers (12 pages maximum) to the conference EasyChair site. Extended abstracts (4–5 pages) may also be submitted, although preference will be given to full papers.

    Papers must be submitted in Portable Document Format (PDF); the revised version of manuscripts must be prepared in LaTeX, using the EPTCS style.

    Papers must not have been submitted simultaneously to other conferences or workshops with published proceedings. Accepted papers must be presented at the workshop. The proceedings will be published as a volume of Electronic Proceedings in Theoretical Computer Science (EPTCS) before the workshop. Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit revised and extended versions of their papers, subject to additional review, for publication in a special issue of the International Journal of Unconventional Computing (IJUC).

    Important Dates


    Submission deadline: 24 March 2016
    Notification of acceptance: 13 May 2016
    Final version of papers due: 3 June 2016

    Registration


    PC2016 is part of the broader UCNC conference and all costs are included in registration for the primary conference. We thus invite you to register for the conference here: http://www.ucnc2016.org/.
