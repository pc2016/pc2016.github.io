    The 7th International Workshop on Physics and Computation (PC2016) will be held as a satellite workshop during UCNC 2016 from 11–15 July 2016.

    We invite the submission of original papers or extended abstracts in all areas of Physics and Computation. Typical, but not exclusive, topics include:

    • Axiomatisation of physics: completeness, decidability, reduction
    • Dynamical systems: computability, complexity
    • Hypercomputation
    • Molecular computation and reaction-diffusion models
    • Multiple-substrate computation
    • Novel models of computation
    • Philosophy of physics and computation
    • (Physical) hurch-Turing thesis
    • Quantum computation and information
    • Quantum logics
    • Randomness: quantum, dynamical & physical randomness
    • Relativistic computation
    • Theory of measurement: axiomatisation, complexity

    Invited Speakers


    Gilles Dowek (Inria & ENS Cachan)

    Important Dates


    Submission deadline: 24 March 2016
    Notification of acceptance: 13 May 2016
    Final version of papers due: 3 June 2016

    Submission


    Authors are invited to submit original papers (12 pages maximum) to the workshop EasyChair site. Extended abstracts (4–5 pages) may also be submitted, although preference will be given to full papers. Please see the submissions page for detailed formatting and submission instructions.

    Proceedings


    The proceedings will be published as a volume of Electronic Proceedings in Theoretical Computer Science (EPTCS) before the workshop. Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit revised and extended versions of their papers, subject to additional review, for publication in a special issue of the International Journal of Unconventional Computing (IJUC).
